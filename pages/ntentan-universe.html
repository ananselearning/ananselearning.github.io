A world to explore, build, and return to.

Ntentan Universe is the living world at the heart of Ananse Learning. It is where our characters, stories, and maker challenges come together.

The name comes from Ananse Ntentan, the spider's web: an Adinkra symbol of intelligence, creativity, and the idea that everything worth knowing is connected to something else. That is the world we are building: a universe where a story becomes a design challenge, a food tradition becomes a research journey, and a character you meet in a book turns up again somewhere unexpected.

Ntentan Universe begins where our physical products leave off. Every poster, flashcard, storybook, and workbook carries a thread that leads here. Children follow those threads at their own pace, in their own order, guided by curiosity rather than a curriculum. What they find, make, and imagine along the way is entirely their own.

Our universe begins at age 3. Where it ends is up to you.
