A world to explore, build, and return to.

Ntentan Universe is the living world at the heart of Ananse Learning, where characters, stories, and maker challenges come together.

The name comes from Ananse Ntentan, the spider's web: an Adinkra symbol of intelligence, creativity, and the idea that everything worth knowing is connected to something else.

Ntentan Universe begins where physical products leave off. Every poster, flashcard, storybook, and workbook carries a thread that leads here. Children follow those threads at their own pace, in their own order, guided by curiosity rather than a curriculum.

Our universe begins at age 3. Where it ends is up to you.

Target app launch window: July 2026 to December 2027.
