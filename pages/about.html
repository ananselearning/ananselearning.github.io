Who We Are

Ananse Learning is a cultural universe woven from Ghanaian heritage. We create books, workbooks, posters, and tools that help children see themselves in what they learn and help everyone experience the craft and depth of Ghanaian culture.

Our products are cultural artefacts: they live on walls, sit on shelves, and pass between hands.

The Web We Weave From

Our name honours Ananse the spider: a figure of wisdom, storytelling, and creative problem-solving in Akan folklore. Our approach, the Ananse Ntentan Exploration framework, is named after the spider's web — a symbol of intelligence, creativity, and the interconnectedness of all knowledge.

It weaves eight pathways: Architecture, Art & Design, Food & Culture, Literature, History, Sustainability, Research, and Technology. Every product we make lives somewhere inside that web.

The World We are Weaving Next

We are building Ntentan Universe: a living digital layer where characters and stories connect, where a poster becomes a portal, and a book becomes the beginning of something a child builds themselves.

We begin with children, but the world we are building belongs to everyone who carries this culture, or is meeting it for the first time.

Mission & Vision

We weave culturally rooted stories, experiences, and products that deepen cultural, environmental, and creative literacy because we believe Ghanaian culture deserves to be a shaping force in global education and design.
