Who We Are

Ananse Learning is a universe woven from Ghanaian culture, stories and heritage. We create artefacts: storybooks, workbooks, posters, and tools that help everyone experience the craft and depth of Ghanaian culture.

Our products live on walls, sit on shelves, and pass between hands.

The Web We Weave From

Our name honours Ananse the spider: a figure of wisdom, storytelling, and creative problem-solving in Akan folklore. Our approach, the Ananse Ntentan Exploration framework, is named after the spider's web — a symbol of intelligence, creativity, and the interconnectedness of all knowledge.

It weaves eight pathways: Architecture, Art & Design, Food & Culture, Literature, History, Sustainability, Research, and Technology. Every product we make lives somewhere inside that web.
