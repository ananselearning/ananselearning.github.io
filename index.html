“Mpanyin se: Tete wɔ bi ka. Tete wɔ bi kyerɛ.”

“The elders say: The past has something to say. The past has something to teach.”



My name is Oluman-Boogie-Annan, aka Smooth like Serekye, for indeed, I am smooth like silk. I am a legend, and Ananse (yes, the one and only) is my great-great-great-grandfather. Ananse is a shapeshifter. He crossed oceans, survived the darkest periods in history, and still showed up on the other side, telling stories. His resilience and power run through me.

I have many lessons to teach and many tales to tell, and you may find me and my children: Mansa the lioness, Anansewaa the giraffe, and Brempong the bear in books or posters. This is a universe you will love returning to.
